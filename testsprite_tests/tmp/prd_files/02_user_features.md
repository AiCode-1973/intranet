# User Features & Functional Requirements

## 1. Authentication & Security
- Secure login with CPF and hashed passwords.
- Role-based access control (Admin vs. Regular User).
- Password recovery and reset functionality.

## 2. Institutional Agenda
- Unified calendar for meetings, celebrations, and important dates.
- Resource reservation system (Projector and Notebook).
- **Automation**: Automatic IT ticket generation when hardware is reserved.

## 3. Human Resources (RH)
- **Digital Documents**: Secure delivery and viewing of paystubs and contracts.
- **Clock-in (Ponto)**: Digital time tracking with supervisor validation.
- **Direct Messaging**: One-way communication from RH to specific employees.

## 4. IT Support & Knowledge Base
- **Ticketing System**: Submit, track, and manage IT support requests.
- **Articles**: Searchable library of tutorials and technical guides.
- **Hardware Integration**: Visibility of upcoming equipment reservations for IT staff.

## 5. Education & Training
- **Course Catalog**: Categorized online courses with video lessons.
- **Assessments**: Integrated exams/quizzes to validate learning.
- **Certificates**: Automatic generation of completion certificates.

## 6. Maintenance & Equipment Requests (CEH)
- Service orders for infrastructure repairs.
- Specialized requests for hospital equipment (CEH).

## 7. Communication
- **Mural**: Digital billboard for institutional announcements.
- **Messaging**: Internal system for inter-departmental communication.
- **Birthdays**: Listing of staff birthdays.
