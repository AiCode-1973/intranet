# Project Overview: APAS Intranet

## Purpose
The APAS Intranet is a centralized internal management system designed to streamline communication, resource management, and administrative tasks for a healthcare-oriented organization (APAS).

## Target Audience
- **General Employees**: Access to institutional news, personal documents (paystubs), training courses, and internal messaging.
- **IT Department**: Manage hardware/software support tickets and technical documentation.
- **Human Resources (RH)**: Manage employee records, time tracking (ponto), and institutional policies.
- **Maintenance/Infrastructure**: Handle facility and equipment maintenance requests.
- **Administrators**: Full system control, user permissions, and auditing.

## Key Goals
1. Centralize institutional information and communication.
2. Digitalize HR processes like paystub delivery and time tracking.
3. Streamline internal service requests (IT, Maintenance, CEH).
4. Provide a platform for internal training and professional development.
